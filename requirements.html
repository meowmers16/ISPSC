Admission Requirements

Requirements for 2nd. Semester AY 2021-2022:

*Copy of the approved schedule with QR Code.

*Vaccination Card for fully and partially vaccinated individuals. If coming from Alert Lever 3 area, present also a Negative Antigen result taken within the last 24 hours.

*School ID / Government-issued ID / Certificate of Residence from your Barangay.

*Face Mask (No face mask, No entry).

*Partially vaccinated and unvaccinated (Alert Level 2 only) students are required to use face shield in addition to face mask and minor students shall be accompanied by a fully-vaccinated adult. If coming from Alert Level 3 area, present a Negative Antigen (NA) result taken within the last 24 hours. (No face mask, face shield and/or Negative Antigen, No Entry).

*Bring your own ballpen, and alcohol for disinfection of hands.

High School Graduate of the Old Basic Curriculum (2015 and below)

*High School Report Card (Form 138).

*Certificate of Good Moral Character.

*Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) formerly NSO.

*2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag (ex. DELA CRUZ, Juan)

Senior High School Graduate (2016 and above):

*SF 9 (Senior HIgh School Report Card).

*Certificate of Good Moral Character.

*Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) formerly NSO.

*2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag (ex. DELA CRUZ, Juan)

ALS/A&E Passers (Secondary Level):

*ALS Rating (PASSED).

*Certificate indicating that the student is eligible for admission to college.

*Certificate of Good Moral Character.

*Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) formerly NSO

*2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag (ex. DELA CRUZ, Juan)

Transferees (In-bound):

*Transfer Credentials: Certificate of Final Grades, Certificate of Good Moral Character, Transfer Certificate

*Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) formerly NSO.

*2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag (ex. DELA CRUZ, Juan).

Cross-enrollee (In-bound from other school):

*Permit to Cross-enroll

Returning Students:

*Certificate of Final Grades or class cards from previous semester

Transferees (In-bound):

*Transfer Certificate

*Certificate of General Weighted Average (GWA).

*Official Transcript of Record.

*Birth Certificate or if married woman Marriage Contract issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) formerly NSO.

*2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag (ex. DELA CRUZ, Juan).
