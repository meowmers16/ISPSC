Admission Requirements

Requirements for 2nd Semester AY 2021-2022:

  • Copy of the approved schedule with QR Code.
  • Vaccination Card for fully and partially vaccinated individuals. For Alert Level 3 areas, present a Negative Antigen result taken within the last 24 hours.
  • School ID, Government-issued ID, or Certificate of Residence from your Barangay.
  • Face Mask (No face mask, No entry).
  • Additional requirements for partially vaccinated and unvaccinated students include a face shield. For Alert Level 3, present a Negative Antigen result.

High School Graduate of the Old Basic Curriculum (2015 and below)

  • High School Report Card (Form 138).
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character.
  • Birth Certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA).
  • 2 pcs. passport size, white background, with name tag.
