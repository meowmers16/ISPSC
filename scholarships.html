    Scholarship Application Procedure:

    A. Fill up the application forms available at the Office of the Scholarship Coordinator.

    B. Present the following together with the duly accomplished application form:

  • Photocopy of high school card (for freshman applicants)
  • Report of grades issued by the Registrar (for sophomore to senior student applicants)
  • Photocopy of present registration form
  • Certificate of Indigence
